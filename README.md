# 📊 Wavecon Telecom – 5G Impact Analysis

## Overview

This repository contains a business analysis project completed as part of my **Data Analyst Internship at AtliQ Technologies** through the **Codebasics Data Analytics Bootcamp 5.0**.

The project evaluates the impact of Wavecon Telecom's **5G launch** on revenue, customer behavior, recharge plan performance, and city-wise growth, with actionable business recommendations.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze revenue before and after the 5G launch.
- Evaluate key business KPIs.
- Identify top and underperforming recharge plans.
- Perform city-wise performance analysis.
- Recommend strategies to improve business performance.

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue remained largely stable after the 5G rollout.
- ARPU increased, while active users declined.
- Long-validity, high-data plans generated the highest revenue.
- Several older plans underperformed and were discontinued.
- Lucknow showed the strongest growth, while Delhi and Ahmedabad need attention.
